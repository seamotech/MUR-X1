Welcome to the documentation page for the MUR-X1 underwater robot project. This document provides an overview of the system’s core capabilities, technical specifications, functional configuration, and typical application scenarios, enabling users to quickly understand the platform’s overall design and intended use.

## Project Overview

MUR-X1 is a compact underwater robotic platform designed for underwater operations and scientific research applications. It features an open software architecture, stable motion control performance, and flexible hardware expandability, making it suitable for underwater inspection, research experiments, public safety operations, and customized industry tasks.

## Key Features

- Open software ecosystem based on ROS for secondary development and system integration
- Support for real-time debugging and operational status monitoring to improve maintenance efficiency
- Integrated AI vision capability with plug-and-play extensibility
- Flexible hardware expansion interfaces for a wide range of mission payloads

## Technical Specifications

### Physical Specifications

- **Dimensions (L × W × H)**: 562 mm × 338 mm × 194 mm
- **Weight without payload (including ballast and battery)**: 10 kg
- **Weight without payload (excluding ballast and battery)**: 9.5 kg
- **Pressure hull structural material**: Marine-grade corrosion-resistant aluminum alloy
- **End cap material**: Marine-grade corrosion-resistant aluminum alloy
- **Buoyancy material**: Rigid polyurethane foam
- **Main sealed cabin dimensions**: Ø133 mm × 400 mm

### Performance Parameters

- **Maximum rated operating depth**: 100 m
- **Maximum forward speed**: 1 m/s
- **Thruster configuration**: 4 horizontal, 2 vertical
- **Maximum thrust (forward direction)**: 20 N
- **Maximum thrust (vertical direction)**: 18 N

### Imaging and Lighting System

- **Main camera resolution**: 1920 × 1080
- **External auxiliary camera resolution**: 1920 × 1080
- **Lens focal length**: 3.6 mm
- **Camera tilt adjustment**: Manually adjustable
- **Lighting output**: 1500 lm

### Sensors and Power System

- **Sensors**: AHRS (IMU and magnetic compass), depth sensor, leak detection, voltage coulomb meter
- **Battery type**: Lithium-ion battery
- **Battery capacity**: 88 Wh
- **Typical endurance**: Approx. 1 hour (normal operation) / Approx. 3 hours (low-power operation)

### Communication and Control

- **Communication tether**: High-strength neutrally buoyant tether (optional lengths available)
- **Host computer connection**: Wi-Fi / Wired network

## Application Scenarios

MUR-X1 can be widely applied in the following fields:

- Underwater aquaculture and precision agriculture
- Underwater infrastructure inspection and maintenance
- Public safety and emergency response
- Marine scientific research and experimental missions
